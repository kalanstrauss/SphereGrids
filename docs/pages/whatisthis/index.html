ROADMAP

The mission of The FIN is to provide extrinsic value for our collectors and community, beyond that of physical goods. We are about community, utility, and running our ecosystem as transparent as possible, on the blockchain. As the universe grows, more utility will be added.

1) Carefully curated ~rare~ collectibles by emerging and prominent artists.

2) The FIN NFT collection.

3) Creation of a virtual gallery, where we will host exhibitions alongside The Latent Space.

4) Site-specific collectibles with the use of AR.

5) Creation of our own token that will be the core of everything in The FIN universe and will be distributed to collectors. The token will allow utility, governance and special access opportunities.

6) Creation of a DAO that will be accessible to our collectors.
